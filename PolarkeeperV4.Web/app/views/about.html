About Polarkeeper

Polarkeeper is developed to fill the gap between Polar Personal Trainer and Runkeeper.

For who?

People using their Polar devices with Polar Personal Trainer simply can't synchronise their data with the services they would like. We try to make that happen for them. Simply connect to your Runkeeper account and start synchronising!


Future?

We are currently developing a system to synchronise your services in an even better way. By looking at your Runkeeper statistics, we will try and match the activities done with your Polar Personal Trainer. In that manner, we will we able to give you an exact overview of uploaded and non-uploaded activities.

Later on, we plan on integrating with more services (Strava, Nike, Endomondo, ...).
